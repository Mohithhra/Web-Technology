OUR STORY

From A Garage
To A Movement

It started in 2016 with a 400 sq ft garage, two barbells, and an unshakeable belief that Puducherry deserved a world-class fitness experience.

Our founder, Arjun Sharma, had just returned from competing nationally in powerlifting. He saw a gap — gyms that were either too commercial or too basic. So he built something different.

Today, IronForge is home to 500+ members across all walks of life — from first-time gym-goers to competitive athletes. Every single one of them is part of our story.

OUR JOURNEY

Built One Rep At A Time

2016

The Beginning

IronForge opens in a 400 sq ft garage with 2 barbells, a squat rack and a dream.

2018

First 100 Members

We hit 100 active members and moved to our first proper facility with 3,000 sq ft of floor space.

2020

Going Digital

Launched online coaching platform during the pandemic. Helped 200+ members train from home.

2022

Premium Expansion

Expanded to 8,000 sq ft. Added boxing ring, turf zone, recovery suite and nutrition lab.

2024

Tech Platform Launch

Launched IronForge digital platform — workout builder, nutrition tracker, progress analytics and community feed.

2025

500+ Members Strong

Today IronForge is Puducherry's #1 gym with 500+ members, 20+ elite coaches and a thriving community.
